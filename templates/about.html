About Us

Welcome to WeatherToday, your reliable source for up-to-date and accurate weather information.
Whether you're planning a trip or simply curious about the forecast, we are here to provide you with the latest weather data from around the world.

Who We Are

At WeatherToday, we are passionate about making weather information accessible to everyone.
Our goal is to deliver real-time weather updates, news, forecasts, and other essential data in a user-friendly format.
With a simple and intuitive interface, you can easily find the information you need, from temperature and humidity to wind speed and visibility.

Our Technology

We use the power of OpenWeather API to provide accurate and reliable weather data. OpenWeather collects data from multiple sources, including weather stations, satellites, and meteorological models, ensuring that we offer the most up-to-date and accurate information.
